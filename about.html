About This Website

BlueImg is a visual discovery website built for clean browsing, fast navigation, and creative inspiration.

Inspired by the minimal flow of Unsplash-style interfaces, BlueImg focuses on images first while keeping the dashboard, explore feed, and utility pages simple to use across all screen sizes.
